CI-TRACS Project Description

The CI-TRACS program responds to the need for Climate Science (CLS) research workforce development in the area of CI. Without sophisticated CI skills CLS in the Hawaii-Pacific region is fundamentally limited with concomitant impacts upon our ability to monitor, predict and mitigate the impacts of climate change on island locales.
